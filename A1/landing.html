DEGANVY, UK

Experience the charming coastal beauty of Deganwy in North Wales. With its medieval castle ruins, stunning marina, and panoramic views of the Conwy Estuary, this picturesque town offers the perfect blend of history and natural beauty. Enjoy fresh seafood at waterfront restaurants or explore the nearby Snowdonia National Park.

WESTERN DESERT, EGYPT

Journey through the golden sands of Egypt's Western Desert, home to stunning white chalk rock formations and hidden oases. Explore the surreal landscapes of the White Desert, camp under star-filled skies, and discover ancient caravan routes used for centuries by Bedouin tribes. A true adventure for those seeking the extraordinary.
